Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 17, 2026

Kashf Lens is a 100% offline application. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data whatsoever.

1. Data Collection

Kashf Lens does not collect any personal information. The app operates entirely on your device without any network connections. No data is ever sent to external servers.

2. Sensor Data

The app accesses the following iPhone sensors solely for on-device analysis:

  • Magnetometer — to detect magnetic field anomalies near walls and surfaces
  • Microphone — to perform vibro-acoustic analysis when you tap on a surface (audio is processed in real-time and never recorded or stored)
  • Camera — to perform AI visual analysis of wall surfaces (images are processed in real-time and never recorded or stored)
  • Motion sensors (accelerometer/gyroscope) — to assist with vibration detection and device orientation

All sensor data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted, stored permanently, or shared with any third party.

3. Bluetooth

The app may request Bluetooth access to connect to optional external sensors (e.g., ESP32-based metal detectors). Bluetooth data stays local to your device and is not transmitted to any external service.

4. Data Storage

Scan results and room mappings are stored locally on your device only. This data is not backed up to any cloud service by the app. You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the application.

5. Third-Party Services

Kashf Lens does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data collection services. There are no SDKs that transmit data externally.

6. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app does not collect any information from anyone.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
